The eye catcher on the bottle is Red star on a blue cap. All over the world, S.Pellegrino's red star is the symbol of the perfect expression of the Italian way of living. A perfect symbol for a leader sparkling mineral water with international premium positioning and exceptional quality, the star is an ancient emblem of Italy since 26 centuries.

The brand's Italian origins and its close bounds with its territory are expressed in its shoulder label, which has a unique, distinctive aesthetic expression represented by The Casino of San Pellegrino Terme. The reproduction of this beautiful Liberty-style building, tighter with the foundation date of S.Pellegrino brand and bottling company, are emblematic in linking S.Pellegrino to its Italian origins and great historical heritage

With its Belle Epoque charm and flair, distinctive watermark and – most of all- its central red star, S.Pellegrino 's cyan label are utterly unmistakable on the labels of the best restaurant around the world. Always accompanied by the contrast with green color of the glass – a unique characteristic that has always associated S.Pellegrino sparkling mineral water to the world of great wines- the first labels were already written in four languages, testifying to the immediate international success of such a unique, exceptional product.
